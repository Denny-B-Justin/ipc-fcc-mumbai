Message from Our Pastor

You’re welcome here—just as you are

Dear family and friends,

We’re glad you’re here. Whether you’re exploring faith for the first time, returning after a long season, or looking for a church home, we want you to know this: you belong.

Our church is a community of ordinary people learning to follow Jesus—growing through worship, prayer, Scripture, and loving one another in practical ways. We are not perfect, but we are committed to walking together with sincerity, humility, and hope.

When you visit, you can expect a warm welcome, Christ-centered teaching, and space to meet people and take your next step—at your pace. If you’d like to speak with someone, request prayer, or learn more about joining our fellowship, we would be honored to help.

With love and prayers,
Pastor Franklin Francis
